This is Fall Guys

A new and entertaining game full of adeventures! In Fall Guys you can enjoy many game modes in which you play against other players online to achieve victory. Test your skills and have fun, but don't forget to reach the crown before everyone else! You can install it in consoles (PS4, XBOX One) or you can also find it on Steam in case you're a PC player

Play with your friends

The most fun and amazing feature of Fall Guys is that you can join groups with your friends to play compete against each other and laugh together, which is easy and fast. You may also like to play it on your own and meet new players in every match. Fall Guys is a game designed to play with everyone in the family, as it is exciting and children-friendly

Lots of levels

The game is constantly evolving since it was first released, and currently it has more than 12 different levels that you may encounter when you play. Its great variety makes the game lively and makes our players stay and tell others about the great time they've had. Our goal is to keep the game fresh and up-to-date with the expectatives of each season

Customize yourself

Do you like to stand out in the real world and wear whatever you enjoy? Well, that's perfectly possible in Fall Guys, because you'll have even more options of clothing for your character than you have in your wardrobe. As you get experience playing, you level up and with each new level you unlock new fancy and colorful clothes to fit your character
